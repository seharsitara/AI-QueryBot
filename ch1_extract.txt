1.1 Introduction
QueryBot is an AI-powered web platform designed to simplify how users interact
with digital documents. In todayΓÇÖs digital world, individuals, students, and
organizations handle a large number of files such as PDFs, Word documents, reports,
and manuals. Manually reading long documents to find specific information is time-
consuming and inefficient. QueryBot aims to solve this problem by allowing users to
upload documents and ask questions in natural language. The AI system understands
the content and responds with accurate, context-based answers. This platform will
serve as a single solution for document search, automated summaries, and intelligent
content extraction for students, teachers, businesses, and professionals.
1.2 Problem Statement
With the rapid digitalization in the World and growing reliance on online learning,
businesses, and documentation, people struggle to extract information quickly from
large documents. Many users face the following challenges:
∩é╖ Lack of a reliable platform for AI-based document searching
∩é╖ Difficulty in reading long research papers, reports, or contracts
∩é╖ Time wasted locating specific information inside documents
∩é╖ No local platform that supports multi-format document processing
∩é╖ Students and professionals often need summaries or explanations but do not
have AI assistance
There is a need for an online AI system in Pakistan that can intelligently process
documents, answer user queries, generate summaries, and improve accessibility to
information.
1.3 Project Goals & Objectives
The primary goal is to develop an AI-based web system that processes uploaded
documents and provides fast, accurate, and human-like responses by understanding
document content.

b. To allow users to upload multiple file formats (PDF, DOCX, TXT).
c. To integrate AI for generating summaries and answering questions.
d. To support multi-document analysis and document comparison.

1.5 Methodology
1.5.1 Chosen Methodology
For this project, we have selected the Agile Software Development Model. Agile is an
iterative and flexible approach that divides the project into small manageable cycles
called sprints. Each sprint involves planning, designing, developing, testing, and
reviewing a small portion of the system. Agile is most suitable for QueryBot because
the project involves AI-based features that may require adjustments based on testing
accuracy, user feedback, and model improvements. The flexibility of Agile allows
continuous refinement of the chatbot responses, document processing pipeline, and
user interface. Agile ensures smooth collaboration, early testing, continuous
improvement, and rapid adaptation to new requirements. Each iteration will help us
improve accuracy, fix issues quickly, and deliver a more reliable and intelligent
document processing system.

1.5.2 Available Methodology
Other available methodologies rather than the waterfall model include:
a. Waterfall Model
Waterfall is a linear model in which each phase must be completed before moving
to the next. It is suitable for projects with stable requirements. However, for
QueryBot, we expect iterative refinement, especially in AI features, so Waterfall
is not ideal.
b. Scrum Framework
Scrum is a subset of Agile and works in short sprints with defined roles like
Product Owner and Scrum Master. Although highly effective, it requires
specialized roles and continuous stakeholder involvement. Since our project team
is small, pure Scrum may add unnecessary complexity.
c. Incremental Development Model
This model builds software in small increments. However, unlike Agile,
increments are delivered in fixed sequences and do not support frequent
requirement changes. QueryBot requires improvements based on testing and
feedback, so Incremental is not the best choice.
1.5.3 Reasons for Chosen Methodology
The following are the reasons leading to the chosen methodology:
a. Flexibility and Adaptability:
Agile allows us to modify or improve AI responses, embeddings, and document
analysis techniques during development.
b. Iterative Improvement:
Each sprint helps test the system early, identify issues, and refine features like
semantic search, chatbot UI, and document processing.
c. Faster Testing and Feedback:
We can test small modules quickly, such as file upload, embeddings generation,
or chatbot responses, and improve them without waiting for the whole system to
finish.
d. Better Risk Management:
AI projects often involve uncertainty in output quality. Agile enables us to handle
risks early by experimenting in short cycles.

1.6 Definitions, Acronyms and Abbreviations
1.6.1 Definitions
i. Document Processing
The automated method of reading, analyzing, and extracting information from
digital documents such as PDFs, Word files, and text files.
ii. Embeddings
A numerical representation of text used by AI models to understand meaning,
context, and similarity within documents.
iii. Semantic Search
A search technique that understands the meaning behind words, allowing the
system to find contextually relevant answers instead of simple keyword matches.
iv. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
A field of Artificial Intelligence that enables computers to understand, interpret,
and generate human language in a meaningful way.
v. AI Chatbot
An intelligent system that interacts with users through text, answering questions
and providing information based on document content.
vi. Vector Database
A specialized database used to store and retrieve embeddings for fast and
accurate similarity-based searching.
vii. Document Ingestion
The process of uploading, reading, and converting documents into
machine- understandable formats for further processing.
viii. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

2.1 Stakeholders Characteristics
For QueryBot, the stakeholders include all individuals and groups who interact with,
benefit from, or influence the AI-based document processing system.
1. Primary Users (Students, Professionals, Researchers)
These users rely on QueryBot to extract information from large documents
quickly. Their needs include fast document uploads, accurate question answering,
automated summaries, and context-aware insights. Their feedback plays a major
role in improving system accuracy, response quality, and overall usability.
Training, FAQ support, and user guidance will ensure smooth adoption across
varying technical skill levels.
2. Organizations & Institutions (Secondary Stakeholders)
Universities, companies, and research teams benefit from QueryBotΓÇÖs ability to
process reports, policies, manuals, and research material. They require secure
document handling, collaborative features, and reliable performance for large files.
Their evaluation is based on system accuracy, integration capability, and
improvements in productivity.
3. Project Team
The project team includes AI/ML engineers, backend developers, frontend
developers, database specialists, and UI/UX designers. Their tasks involve
designing models, integrating vector databases, developing embeddings pipelines,
and ensuring that QueryBot answers accurately and efficiently. Success requires
coordination, resource availability, proper tools, and strong communication
among team members to handle iterative improvements and scalability needs.
2.2 DOMAIN REQUIREMENTS
Domain requirements describe the essential functionalities and constraints for an AI-
based document processing system.
1. Document Interaction Requirements
The solution must understand user questions and retrieve content from documents,
including PDF, DOCX, and text files. It should provide precise, context-aware
answers based on semantics rather than keywords. The system must handle
complex queries regarding definitions, summaries, tables, and structured content.
2. AI & NLP Processing Requirements
QueryBot must utilize advanced NLP techniques for embeddings, semantic search,
summarization, and intent detection. It should maintain accuracy across different
document types, support domain-specific vocabulary, and adapt as more
document are uploaded.
